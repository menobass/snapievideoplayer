What is 3speak?

3speak is a decentralized video platform built on the Hive blockchain. Unlike traditional video platforms, 3speak gives creators full ownership of their content and rewards them directly through cryptocurrency.

This player is the next generation video player for 3speak, designed to provide a fast, reliable, and feature-rich viewing experience. Videos are stored on IPFS (InterPlanetary File System), making them censorship-resistant and permanently accessible.

Built with modern web technologies including Video.js, this player supports both the legacy 3speak video collection and the new direct-upload system for frontends.
